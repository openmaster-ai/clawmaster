# Platform Rules

## `xhs`

- Lead with one strong headline sentence.
- Keep copy modular and card-friendly.
- Prefer 4-8 short sections over one long wall of text.
- End with a light CTA or takeaway, not a hard sell.
- Image plan:
  - 1 cover
  - 2-6 supporting panels

## `wechat`

- Start with a clear title and a short dek.
- Use section headings that read well as an article outline.
- Keep transitions explicit and calm.
- Assume the reader will scroll, so longer paragraphs are acceptable.
- Image plan:
  - 1 hero image
  - optional inline supporting visuals

## Shared Rules

- Prefer preserving factual meaning over maximizing style.
- Surface uncertainty instead of inventing missing details.
- Save one platform per artifact directory.
